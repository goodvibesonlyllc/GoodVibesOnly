Tools & Resources

We offer a wake up call and SMS service that'll ring your number to have you notified as soon as any random drops occur. In certain scenarios such as YEEZYSUPPLY and any shock drops, this is where this tool will thrive. We also have an AIO bot that generates shopify accounts, produces slightly different variants for your address, and more.

Early Information

Acquire the most sought after hype releases with information no ordinary person will get. Ranging from subdomain changes to published products on site, we'll keep you in touch with it all.

24/7 Support

Our staff will keep you tidy pre-release. We'll work around the clock to make sure you're perfectly situated. "One on one" support is nice, however having multiple staff help sounds a lot better.

Guides

With our frequently updated guides, you'll be able to easily search for all the answers to your questions. Ranging from Shopify bookmarks to website Wishlists, everything will be easily accessible.

Sitelists per Release

For every release, we will provide you with an organized sitelist. From categories for manual users to experienced users, we'll help you ignore the unmanageable sites and save you the extra minutes of sleep.

High End Monitors

Upon entry, access will be granted to our monitors. We monitor websites that include and are not limited to: Shopify, Supreme, Nike and Nike Desktop, Adidas, Footsites, Hibbett, Ssense, and Frenzy.

Committed Support Team

Whether a beginner or a truly advanced reseller, we'll keep you neat and tidy before releases. From events ranging from hourly question and answers to fully extensive written guides, you'll be able to acquire the goods without hassle.

Unique links & information

You'll always be prepared for a release. Members will have access to everything ranging from local in-store information to online information from stores ranging from Kith, UNDFTD, Footstores, and many more.

Quick-Tasks

With our monitors, you'll be able to have built in quick-tasks. In short, upon clicking the quick-task feature, your local supported bot will automatically run that specific item that you've clicked which optimizes and enhances checkout speeds dramatically.
